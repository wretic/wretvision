MAIN CRITICISM OF MODERN RESIDENT EVIL

Potential article:

"Resident Evil's Biggest Problem Isn't Horror. It's Repetition."

The issue is not quality.

The issue is structural repetition.

Examples:

RE1 = Mansion
RE0 = Train -> Mansion
Code Veronica = Palace / Mansion
RE7 = House
Village = Castle
Requiem = Estate / Mansion + Laboratory

Recurring patterns:

- Large building.
- Hidden laboratory.
- Cure or vaccine plot.
- Escape sequence.

The surprise disappears.

OUTBREAK REMAKE ANALYSIS

Canon Status:
Canon

Timeline:
Occurs during the Raccoon City incident alongside RE2 and RE3.

Preferred Approach:

Do NOT remake Outbreak and File #2 separately.

Create:

Resident Evil: Outbreak

One complete RE Engine package combining both games.

Personal Assessment:

Concept: 9/10
Execution: 4/10

Strengths:

- Ordinary civilians.
- Multiple perspectives.
- Raccoon City during the outbreak.
- Excellent overall premise.
- Memorable locations.
- Strong survival horror atmosphere.

Weaknesses:

- Clunky controls.
- Frustrating AI.
- Constant repetitive dialogue.
- Dated systems.
- Poor pacing.
- Excessive micromanagement.

Personal take:

I would rather replay Resident Evil 0 a hundred times than replay Outbreak in its current form.

The tragedy of Outbreak is not bad ideas.

The tragedy is excellent ideas trapped inside outdated design.

Potential remake score:
9/10

RESIDENT EVIL 0

Personal Assessment:

The Ecliptic Express is one of the strongest openings in the entire franchise.

Strengths:

- Excellent train setting.
- Strong atmosphere.
- Memorable opening.
- Interesting protagonist pairing.

Weaknesses:

- No item boxes.
- Excessive inventory management.
- Frustrating item dropping system.
- Partner AI issues.
- Backtracking.

Personal take:

The train section should have been a much larger portion of the game.

Unfortunately the game eventually falls back into another mansion-style structure.
