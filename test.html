Silent Hill 2 (2024)

Developer: Bloober Team
Publisher: Konami
Platform: PS5
Genre: Survival Horror

Bloober Team stepping into Silent Hill 2 was either going to be a masterstroke or a disaster, and somehow, they pulled off one of the most faithful, and at times, even transcendent remakes in recent memory.

They didn't just rebuild the game, they respected the silence. The pacing, the dread, the way the town breathes around you, it's all here. The visuals are gorgeous in a way that feels rotten, not polished. Every hallway, every sound cue, every piece of static feels like a scalpel to the brain.

Combat is heavier now, more desperate. James feels less like a man walking through guilt and more like a man being pulled apart by it. The updated voice acting is phenomenal, subtle, tragic, and raw.

This isn't just a remake. It's remembrance.
Silent Hill didn't come back.
It never left.

Wretvision Verdict
9.5 /10
A near perfect resurrection of psychological horror.
Bloober didn't just honor the original, they understood it.
A must-play. In the fog, we're all just searching.
